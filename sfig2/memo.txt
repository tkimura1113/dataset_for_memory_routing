The ratio of signals by the memory-pauto method is named "${topology}_memory_auto_S.dat"

In all the files, the topology is composed of ba, sw, and ke, which stand for the BA, SW, and KE models, and each column expresses the following statistics:

1. the number of generating packets,
2. the ratio of signals,
3. an unbiased standard deviation,
4. the minimum value of the 95% confidence interval,
5. the maximum value of the 95% confidence interval.
